Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Connectivity & Data

Orbital Defender offers a high-performance experience. While the core game logic is fully functional offline, an active internet connection is required to access the 1.5x Star Dust bonus and other ad-supported rewards. We do not collect personal identifiers like names or locations.

2. Advertisements (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to serve ads. If you grant permission via the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt, AdMob may use your device ID to show relevant ads. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will be non-personalized.

3. Third Parties

Our advertising partner is Google AdMob. You can view their privacy practices here: Google Privacy & Terms.
